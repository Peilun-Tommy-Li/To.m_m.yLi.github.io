I am a senior undergraduate studying Computer Science (CS) and Mathematics at Vanderbilt University, where I'm privileged to work under and advised by Professor Thomas Beckers and Professor Glenn Webb.

My current research interests lie at the intersection of applied mathematics, control theory, and machine learning . I am particularly focused on advancing safe learning-based modeling and control of physical systems. My work involves the development of both theoretical frameworks and practical applications of data-driven methodologies for ordinary differential equations (ODE) and partial differential equations (PDE) systems. The primary objectives of my research are to enhance the generalizability, reliability, and interpretability of these approaches.
