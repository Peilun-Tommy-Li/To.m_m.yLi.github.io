Research Highlights

My recent work revolves around brining physics knowledge to learning-based models for generalizability, reliability and interpretability. Specifically, I have been working with physics-informed learning of port-Hamiltonian Systems.

In line with my research theme, I also worked in mathematical biology where I developed a noval age-structured compartmental model for COVID-19 using systems of ordinary differential equations.
